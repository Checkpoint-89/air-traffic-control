# Air Traffic Control Simulator

A comprehensive simulation system for air traffic management that simulates realistic air traffic, generates automated Air Traffic Control (ATC) requests, and evaluates the safety of automated systems compared to human controllers.

## Overview

This simulator will provide:

1. **Air Traffic Simulation**
   - Realistic aircraft movement and flight dynamics
   - Multiple aircraft types and flight plans
   - Weather and environmental conditions

2. **Automated Air Traffic Management**
   - Conflict detection and resolution
   - Automated ATC instruction generation
   - Real-time trajectory prediction

3. **Safety Evaluation**
   - Comparison with human controller decisions
   - Comprehensive safety metrics
   - Performance analysis and reporting
